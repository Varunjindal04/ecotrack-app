## 📌 About the Project

The Carbon Footprint Calculator is a web application that helps users estimate their monthly carbon emissions based on their daily lifestyle habits.

With rising global carbon emissions, it is important for individuals to understand how their everyday activities—such as transportation, energy usage, and food consumption—impact the environment.

This project allows users to:
- Calculate their carbon footprint  
- Understand their environmental impact  
- Get awareness about sustainable living  

---

## 🚀 Features

- 📊 Calculate monthly carbon emissions  
- 🧠 Based on user lifestyle inputs  
- 📈 Visual output of results  
- 🌱 Promotes eco-friendly awareness  
- 🖥️ Simple and user-friendly interface  

---

## ▶️ How to Use

1. Open the app:  
   👉 https://carbonfootprintcalculator.streamlit.app/

2. Fill in your lifestyle details  

3. Navigate through tabs and calculate your carbon footprint  

4. View your results and understand your impact
