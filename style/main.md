# 🌍 Carbon Footprint Calculator

This web application estimates your monthly carbon footprint based on your daily lifestyle habits.

It takes into account factors such as:
- Transportation habits
- Diet type
- Energy consumption
- Waste generation

Using a Machine Learning model, the system predicts your carbon emissions and provides an estimate of how many trees are required to offset your footprint.

---

# 🌱 About EcoTrack System

A carbon footprint measures the total greenhouse gas emissions caused directly or indirectly by an individual’s activities. It helps in understanding the impact of our lifestyle on the environment.

---

# 🌿 Why It Matters

#### 🍃 Climate Impact
Reducing carbon emissions helps in controlling global warming and climate change.

#### 🍃 Resource Conservation
Efficient use of energy and resources promotes sustainability.

#### 🍃 Health Benefits
Lower emissions improve air quality and overall health.

#### 🍃 Sustainable Living
Encourages eco-friendly habits and responsible consumption.
